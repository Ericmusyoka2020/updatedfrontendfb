How to Use the FBGrab

  • Copy the Facebook video link you want to download.
  • Go to the homepage and paste the link in the input box.
  • Click the Open Link button.
  • You will now see two options appear:
      • Download Video
      • Download Audio
  • Select the format you want (Video or Audio).
  • A player will open. Click the three dots (⋮) on the bottom-right of the player.
  • Choose Download to save the file.
Tip:
If you’re using the Facebook app, tap the three dots (⋮) on the bottom-right of the video → choose Copy Link → come back and paste it in the downloader.Any challenge or Problem Feel free to contact us to get more info.
